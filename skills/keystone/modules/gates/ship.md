# Ship Gate

## Purpose
Ensure finalization happens only after completed, verified, reviewed work is ready for delivery.

## Required checks
- Proof gate is satisfied or exceptions are explicit.
- Review gate is satisfied or exceptions are explicit.
- Delivery notes, risks, and human follow-ups are clear.

## Pass condition
The work is ready for handoff, PR, merge, or release preparation.

## Fail action
Stop finalization and route to the missing module or gate.
